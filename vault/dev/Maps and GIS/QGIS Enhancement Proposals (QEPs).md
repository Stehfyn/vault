# QGIS Enhancement Proposals (QEPs)

QGIS's RFC/PEP-equivalent process — each substantial change to the project (new API, breaking change, plugin-system change, governance change) is filed here as an issue with a structured template, debated, then merged or rejected. Useful when you want to know *why* QGIS does something the way it does, or whether a change you want to propose has already been discussed and shot down. The history is also a decent case study in how an open-source GIS community negotiates roadmap priorities between volunteer devs, paid devs (Boundless/Planet/North Road), and the QGIS.org Project Steering Committee.

```text
Browse the open and closed issues for: API-stable plugin hooks, expression
function additions, processing-framework changes, 3D viewer overhauls,
and the migration plans for major Qt or PROJ version bumps. The labels and
PSC tags tell you which are funded vs. speculative.
```
