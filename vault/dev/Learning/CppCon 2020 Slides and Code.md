# CppCon 2020 Slides and Code

Official archive of slides, sample code, and speaker materials from CppCon 2020 (the year the conference went fully online due to the pandemic). Talks worth referencing later from this year include Klaus Iglberger's design-patterns track, Jason Turner's "Best Practices Every C++ Programmer Needs To Follow", Bjarne Stroustrup's keynote on "The Beauty and Power of 'Primitive' C++", and the C++20-specific talks introducing concepts, coroutines, modules, and `std::ranges` to working programmers. Each talk lives in its own subdirectory with a PDF/PPTX of slides and (where the speaker provided them) compilable samples.
