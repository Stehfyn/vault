# InvestIndex — Beginner Investing Guide

Markdown-and-asset source for "A Beginner's Guide to Investing" — a free, evidence-based personal-finance textbook aimed at US retail investors who've never bought a security. Thirteen sections + advanced topics, ~3 hours of reading: index funds, account types (taxable/IRA/Roth/401k/HSA), tax considerations, debt repayment ordering, insurance and estate basics, the behavioral pitfalls (sequence-of-returns risk, recency bias, panic-selling). Bogleheads-school — passive, broadly diversified, low-cost — not a stock-picking guide. The repo is GitHub Pages source; the rendered site is the actual reading surface. Worth bookmarking simply because most "investing 101" content on the open web is affiliate-driven or wrong.
